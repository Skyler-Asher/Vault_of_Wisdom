⚙️ Upgrade Condition (core rule)
---

1️⃣ **Consistent execution**  
The standard is lived repeatedly over time.

2️⃣ **Reduced internal resistance**  
Behavior happens with less friction or hesitation.

3️⃣ **Stability under pressure**  
The standard holds during difficulty, not just comfort.

4️⃣ **Temporal reliability**  
Execution persists across days/weeks, not bursts.

5️⃣ **Qualitative refinement** ⭐  
There is a **clear improvement in how** the standard is embodied  
(e.g., more precise, more aligned, more skillful, more efficient, more integrated).
