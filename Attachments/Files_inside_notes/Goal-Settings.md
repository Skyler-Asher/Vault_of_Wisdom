Getting any start-up business, especially a trading business off the ground will likely require 100% of your **focused energy.**  

GOAL SETTING

Focus on:

3 Annual goals, split into;

3 goals per Quarter, split into;

3 goals per Month, split into;

3 milestones per Week, split into;

3 high priority actions per Day

The Process:

1. Decide what you want to achieve. 
2. Try different ways of achieving it until you find one that works for you. 
3. Do more of what works. Do less of what doesn't. 
4. Don't stop doing it until it stops working.
5. Repeat. 

PROCESS > OUTCOME

All goals should be **process**, not outcome based.  

**Visualising the end result** is not the fastest method and certainly not the one with the highest chance of working.

It is difficult to match the **big distance in vibration** that exists between you and the desired end result.

**Imagine the process** of moving towards the desired result.

When there is a **smaller vibrational gap** between you and your current objective, it’s naturally accomplished **much easier.**

With visualisation, we give **direction** to our preferred scenario. It allows us to change the line of life.

**Visualise the process.**

PROCRASTINATION

Procrastination is a privilege.

You do not procrastinate when your life is in danger, you just act.

What would less procrastination mean for the future you / your family / causes you care about?

Understand the long-term consequence of inaction. 

Diet / sleep / structure -> if you want to fix it, measure it. 

SOCIAL INFLUENCE

Do not underestimate the **power of social influence on your success** or lack thereof.

Be very careful with **who** you choose to share your goals with. 

Stop asking blind people to proofread **your** vision. 

Showing them _your_ want (desire) makes them aware of _their_ want (lack).

They’d rather not hear about the vision you have, because it **reminds them of the one they’ve lost**.

People will quit on you - you have to get up every day and **make sure you don’t quit on yourself.**

When you do it for **yourself,** there is **no timeline.**

When you do it for **others'** approval, the timeline is always **too close** and right **in your face.**

There is a general **fear** across the trading industry which is primarily **procrastination-**based; those that seek the **acceptance** of friends, family, community because society reflects upon the need for moral support. 

However this is Photon...no matter where you are on your journey, you are fully supported here regardless and we will not judge you for how slow you go, but we will **constructively** criticise when you're making decisions that will negatively affect both your short and long term success. 

TRADING FANTASIES

Humans overestimate what they can achieve in months & underestimate what they can achieve in a few years.

You wouldn't watch a course on Brain Surgery & expect to operate on a patient the next day. 

So don't expect the same with trading. 

Pilot Crash Course = crash landing! 

KEEPING ON

Don't ignore the little wins.

Savour them, they'll keep you going and push you to the next win. 

Monitor all progression, enjoy the milestones, it keeps the inspiration high. 

Keeping on is the hardest battle of all, so each win is important. 

We are created for greatness.

So start **believing and acting** as so.
