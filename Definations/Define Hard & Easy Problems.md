## Hard problems
 that you don't know how to solve - on a timeline you aren't sure you're going to solve them in - all while being unsure if all this will have been for nothing . 




## Easy Problem 
An easy problem is one where the path to a solution is largely known. The methods required are familiar, uncertainty is low, and the remaining unknowns are small. Because of this, focused effort within a short period is usually enough to reach a reliable solution without significant discovery.

## **Medium Problems**  
Problems where the solution method is not yet clear and the time required is uncertain, but you are confident a solution exists and that solving it will be valuable. These problems usually require experimentation and persistent effort, often taking weeks or months to resolve.
