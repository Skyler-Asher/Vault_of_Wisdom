# Exercise _plan

Nice — I made a clean, ready-to-follow **5-on / 2-off Upper/Lower + Full-body plan** using only the exercises you know. Rest days are **not** back-to-back (they’re spread through the week). I included the **Pillow (knock-knee) fix — 5 min × 2 sets** on both Upper and Lower days as you asked.

---

## Weekly layout

Day 1 — Upper A  
Day 2 — Lower A  
Day 3 — Rest / Mobility  
Day 4 — Upper B  
Day 5 — Lower B  
Day 6 — Full-body / Conditioning  
Day 7 — Rest

---

## Daily template (warm-up → main → mobility → cool-down)

**Warm-uq2ns, light dynamic movement.  
**Between sets rest:** 90–120 s (2 min) recommended.  
**Cool-down:** towel shoulder mobility work, neck extensions/rotations, light stretching.

---

## Day 1 — Upper A

- Pillow (knock-knee) fix — **2 sets × 5 min**
    
- Push-ups 3×8–15
    
- Assisted pull-ups 3×max
    
- Pike push-ups 3×6–12
    
- Dead hangs 2×20–40 s
    
- Plank 3×30–60 s
    

## Day 2 — Lower A

- Pillow (knock-knee) fix — **2 sets × 5 min**
    
- Bodyweight squats 4×10–20
    
- Lunges 3×8–12 each leg
    
- Glute bridges 3×12–15
    
- Calf raises 3×15–25
    
- Optional squat hold 2×30–45 s
    

## Day 3 — Rest / Light mobility

- Towel shoulder mobility, neck rotations, easy walk/stretch.
    

## Day 4 — Upper B

- Pillow (knock-knee) fix — **2 sets × 5 min**
    
- Incline push-ups 3×10–15
    
- Diamond / decline push-ups 3×6–12
    
- Assisted pull-ups or dead hangs 3×(assist reps or 20–40 s)
    
- Dumbbell curls 3×8–12
    
- Side plank 3×30 s each side
    

## Day 5 — Lower B

- Pillow (knock-knee) fix — **2 sets × 5 min**
    
- Weighted squats (or heavier bodyweight) 4×6–10
    
- Lunges or reverse lunges 3×8–12 each
    
- Single-leg glute bridges / glute bridges 3×8–12
    
- Calf raises 4×12–20
    
- Short jump rope finisher 3–5 min
    

## Day 6 — Full-body / Conditioning

- 8–12 min jump rope intervals
    
- Circuit ×3: 10 push-ups, 6–8 assisted pull-ups, 15 squats, 45 s plank (minimal rest)
    
- Dead hangs 1–2 × 20–40 s
    
- Mobility cooldown (towel work + neck)
    

## Day 7 — Rest

---

## Progression & tips

- Improve by more reps, less assistance, slower tempo, or added weight.
    
- Keep form first; stop for sharp pain.
    
- If workouts feel long, pick **3–5 core moves** each day and keep sets.
    
- Pillow (knock-knee) fix is deliberately long — treat as focused corrective work; break into manageable intervals if needed.
